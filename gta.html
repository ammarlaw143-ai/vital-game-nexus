Best GTA Online Money Methods (2025)

1. Cayo Perico Heist (Solo)

~$1.5M / 90 min · Best solo heist. Scout Madrazo's Files, take Panther Statue ($1.9M) when active.

2. Cluckin' Bell Farm Raid

~$800K / 50 min · Newer 2024 heist. Less repetitive, scales well in 2-4 player teams.

3. Acid Lab (Passive)

Set up MC business, run Fooligan job. Yields ~$335K per full bar of supplies. AFK-friendly.

4. Auto Shop Contracts

~$200-300K / 45 min · Union Depository Contract pays best. Great solo or with one friend.

5. Diamond Casino Heist

~$1.3M (Diamonds) / 2 hours · Big Con approach with Gruppe Sechs is fastest stealth route.

6. Weekly Bonuses

Always check the Tuesday update — 2x/3x money events rotate weekly. Stack with your main grind.

🌴 Setting: Leonida

A fictionalized Florida — Vice City, the Everglades-inspired Leonida Keys, Port Gellhorn and dense neon coastal nightlife.

👥 Dual Protagonists

Lucia (franchise first female lead) and Jason — a Bonnie-and-Clyde dynamic confirmed in Trailer 1 with on-the-run heist energy.

🎬 Trailer 1 Breakdown

Dropped Dec 2023 — 91M views in 24h. Showcases crowd density, social media feeds, hyperrealistic faces and dynamic weather.

🚗 Vehicle Rumors

Datamines suggest 700+ vehicles at launch, expanded plane roster, and reintroduction of bicycles and jet skis from Vice City Stories.

📱 Living World

Confirmed: in-world social media (Bleeter successor "Vinewood Stories"), live NPC interactions, and dynamic crime witnessing.

🎮 Engine: RAGE 9

Next-gen RAGE engine — wet/dry skin shaders, real-time interior lighting, full underwater swim mechanics returning from San Andreas.

💵 Economy 2.0

Rumored physical currency carry mechanic, robbable ATMs, gas stations, and procedural store interiors across the entire map.

🏖️ Map Size

Leaks suggest ~2x GTA V's land area, with full enterable buildings and the largest Rockstar interior count ever shipped.

🌐 Online Future

GTA Online VI confirmed but launching post-release. Rockstar exploring cross-progression from GTA V Online (TBC).

Most Asked Questions

Will GTA VI come to PC?

Almost certainly — but not at launch. RDR2 and GTA V both followed the same pattern (~12 months later).

Pre-order open?

Not yet. Rockstar never opens pre-orders before a final release date is confirmed. Avoid "pre-order" scam sites.

Will it have crossplay?

Unconfirmed for story mode (single-player). GTA Online VI is heavily rumored to support crossplay across PS5 and Xbox.

Price?

Expected $69.99-$79.99 standard. Special editions likely $99.99+. Take-Two has hinted at "premium pricing".
