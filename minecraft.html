Getting Started · Day 1 Checklist

1. Punch Wood

Get 8+ logs, craft a crafting table, sticks and wooden pickaxe.

2. Mine Stone

Upgrade to stone pickaxe, sword and axe. Grab coal along the way.

3. Kill Sheep

Grab 3 wool + 3 planks for a bed. Sleeping skips the mob-heavy night.

4. Build Shelter

A 3×3 dirt hut works. Place a door and a torch — you survived night one.

Boss & Hostile Mob Guide

Ender Dragon

200 HP · Destroy end crystals with arrows first. Aim for the head with a diamond+ sword during perch. Drops 12,000 XP + a dragon egg.

Wither

300 HP · Spawn in bedrock cage below Y=-30. Bring Strength II potions, golden apples and a Netherite sword. Drops a Nether Star.

Warden

500 HP · Do not fight. Sneak in wool, avoid sculk sensors, block line of sight. One hit deals 30 damage (through Netherite).

Elder Guardian

80 HP · Ocean Monument boss. Bring Night Vision and Water Breathing. Milk cures Mining Fatigue debuff.

Piglin Brute

50 HP · Bastion guard. Gold armor does NOT pacify it. Kite around corners, use enchanted golden apples if cornered.

Ravager + Evoker

Raid enemies. Evoker Vex swarm is the real threat — kill the Evoker first. Drops Totem of Undying.

Redstone Cheat Sheet

Signal Strength

Redstone dust travels max 15 blocks. Use a repeater every 15 blocks to boost back to 15.

Piston vs Sticky Piston

Sticky pulls its block back. Both push up to 12 blocks. Slime and honey blocks propagate movement.

Comparator Modes

Compare mode reads container fullness. Subtract mode outputs main − side signal. Right-click to toggle.

Observer Block

Emits a 1-tick pulse when the block in front changes state. Perfect for crop farms and auto-doors.

Hopper Timing

Hoppers move 1 item every 4 game ticks (2.5/sec). Powered hoppers stop moving items — useful for filters.

Clock Circuits

2 repeaters in a loop = fastest safe clock. Observer facing observer = 2-tick clock but very laggy.
